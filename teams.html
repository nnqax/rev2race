Formula 1 Teams

Formula 1 consists of 10 teams, each fielding two drivers in a battle for both the Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. These teams design and develop their own cars, often in partnership with engine suppliers. The teams play a crucial role in the sport, balancing innovation, strategy, and driver performance to compete at the highest level.
